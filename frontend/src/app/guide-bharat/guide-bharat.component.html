GUIDELINES FOR COSTUMES


  1. Can perform with dance practice saree with makeup or without makeup.
  2. Can perform with dance dress of the respective dance traditions, with makeup
  3. Only juniors and sub-juniors are permitted to wear kurti ( chudidhar or dance dress ).

AUDIO GUIDELINES


  1. Can use pre-recorded audio
  2. Can utilize live orchestra
  3. Guru can sing with shruthi box ( without the need of any orchestra )

NOTE: It doesn't matter whether the music is pre-recorded or live orchestra or Guru's singing . The priority of the Marks is given for the background music with perfect shruthi and laya related to the respective songs ( ITEMS ) & the performers talent of bhaava, abhinaya with perfect laya are taken into much consideration.

VIDEO GUIDELINES


  1. Can record the video at home or stage with a decent background.
  2. Any kind of decorations or flex boards are not allowed in the background.
  3. The background should be simple and neat and should match against the colour of the outfit of the performer.
  4. The audio and video should have a good clarity.
  5. The participant (dancer) should give an introduction about ragam, talam, and composer's (writer) name, the name of the song to be performed and the date of recording also have to be mentioned in the video introduction
  6. Use tripod to avoid shaky videos.

JUDGEMENT


  1. Performances will be judged by senior leading international dance teachers. Judges decision is final
  2. Once registered, only registration number will be used for judgement
  3. Participation Certificates will be given to all the Participants
  4. Certificates will be issued for each and every category & levels
  5. 1st, 2nd 3rd place Certificates will be issued for international/national/state - for each Category & levels

RESULTS ANNOUNCEMENT


  1. All the Prizes will be announced on March 31st 2022.
  2. Performances will be judged by senior leading international dance teachers. Judges decision is final
  3. The pure Gold medal and the pure Silver medal along with the Award will either be given personally at Eluru office (Andhra Pradesh) or Chennai (Tamilnadu) office. (OR) The Award and medal will be distributed in any of our Music & Dance Festival organized by ICMDA (International Carnatic Musicians and Dancers Association ).
