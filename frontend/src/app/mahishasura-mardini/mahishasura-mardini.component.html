GUIDELINES FOR MAHISHASURA MARDINI AIGIRI NANDINI

Who are involved in this world record attempt?


  1. ICMDA is conducting the Aigiri Nandini 10 world records attempt with largest musicians and dancers
  2. In Association with Department of Language and Culture of Govt of India & Govt of Andhra Pradesh
  3. With Blessings of Vijayawada Kanaga Durgamma
  4. Sponsors from all over the world. Anyone can come forward to become a sponsor for this great event

How to Attempt the world record?


  1. Register for the world record attempt
  2. We will send you the Notation, pitch, audio record, video choreography after you register
  3. Participant have to sing or play or dance for the material given by ICMDA
  4. We will share the online link to participate for the official world record attempt
  5. Participant have to do the rehearsal before the official world record attempt and get idea on how to join the online event
  6. Every participant have to join the online event 30 minutes before the official attempt time.

General Guidelines


  1. Aigiri Nandini (Mahishasura Mardini Slokas) should be in Shruthi and Thalam for Music and correct Choreograph, Abhinayas & Mudras for Dance artforms.
  2. Stick to the Stipulated time(IST Time Zone) We have Provided
  3. Announce only Your Name , Artform , City & Country before you start the Performance .
  4. Attire: Traditional Indian outfit (Music: Long skirt for Girls and sari for women & Kurtha or Dhothi for Boys/Men
    Dance: Wear traditional dance dress.)

Audio and Video Guidelines


  1. Use Bluetooth Speaker with Microphone.
  2. Your audio should be free from all background noise and clear for adjudicators and witness to hear
  3. Please do a test practice before the offical attempt.
  4. No karaoke music allowed for this video recording.
  5. Make sure that there will no shake in the video.
  6. Video background can be plain/simple and match against the color of your outfit.
  7. Avoid, white and black colors for video quality purposes.
  8. Angle to center of the performer and close enough to see the vocalist's hand thalam
  9. Video should be in landscape mode
