GUIDELINES FOR MAHISHASURA MARDINI AIGIRI NANDINI

General Guidelines


  1. Aigiri Nandini (Mahishasura Mardini Slokas) should be in Shruthi and Thalam for Music and correct Choreograph, Abhinayas & Mudras for Dance artforms.
  2. Stick to the Stipulated time(IST Time Zone) We have Provided
  3. Announce only Your Name , Artform , City & Country before you start the Performance .
  4. Attire: Traditional Indian outfit (Music: Long skirt for Girls and sari for women & Kurtha or Dhothi for Boys/Men
    Dance: Wear traditional dance dress.)

Audio and Video Guidelines


  1. Use Bluetooth Speaker with Mike.
  2. You can use tala app or mridangam for accompaniment
  3. Your audio should be free from all background noise and clear for judges to hear
  4. Please do a test recording before the final version.
  5. No karaoke music allowed for this video recording.
  6. Use a tripod to shoot so there is NO shake in the video.
  7. Video background can be plain/simple and match against the color of your outfit.
  8. Avoid, white and black colors for video quality purposes.
  9. Angle to center of the performer and close enough to see the vocalist's hand thalam
  10. Only LIVE recording is permitted & date of recording should be mentioned.
  11. Video should be in Landscape only and portrait mode will be disqualified.
