STUDENT'S BENEFITS

  1. TEACHERs PLATFORM will be arranged.
  2. Online & Offline classes will be provided.
  3. You can choose your teacher.
  4. Scholarships will be provided.
  5. Syllabus will be provided by ICMDA Grade examinations under Govt guidelines.
  6. Grade examinations will be conducted all over the world every year.
  7. ICMDA, ISO quality control certification & UASL quality management authorized certificates will be provided for music schools, teachers & students.
