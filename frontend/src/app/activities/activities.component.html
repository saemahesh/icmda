WORKING TOWARDS CONTINUOUS IMPROVING WELFARE OF MUSIC & DANCE COMMUNITY

  • To have fair representation of Carnatic Music / Musicians & Dance / Dancers in Global music & dance festivals in india and abroad.
  • Creating pension schemes / other financial assistance such as medical aid, medical insurance.
    1. Providing financial aid and other financial assistance such as medical aid, medical insurance, pension/financial support, etc to indigent, ailing and senior musicians as decided by the committee.
    2. Providing Grade examination for Talent upcoming artists to promote professional artists & Teachers.
    3. To develop a good & healthy relationship between the Association and Government organizations, music institutions/Sabhas, Rasikaas, Media, and sponsors.
    4. In obtaining travel concessions for flights and trains, as well as baggage allowances for instruments.
    5. To ascertain problems of/between the members, analyze the same and to provide with best solutions for such issues and to sort the same without damaging the professional interests of the members and to create healthy relationship.
    6. To support and nurture new talents by providing references / recommendations / scholarships.
    7. To create a website representing the Carnatic artists fraternity highlighting the contribution of fraternity to the society. This can also be a validation platform to establish the credibility of an artist. To create awareness on Carnatic music & classical Dance Artforms through social media and to highlight the contribution.
    8. Protecting the freedom of musical expression of artistes against any efforts to disrupt Carnatic music performances, and in addition protect the artists from being trolled in social media and affecting the reputation of the art form.
    9. To establish a Museum in remembrance of legendary musicians and to celebrate their contributions to the world of Carnatic music.
    10. To enable the organization to contribute for charity and other social causes
    11. Every year Regularly we organize Competitions &Youth festivals in India & abroad to bring out the new young talents.
    12. To conduct workshops for members to develop fitness, personality, communication, and professional skills.

Mission :

  1. To protect the interests of Carnatic Music & Classical Dance artists irrespective of age, caste, creed, sex, nationality, etc. and to provide them with avenues and opportunities & more benefits , both monetary and psychological. Perform activities that contribute towards growth and preservation of the art forms.
