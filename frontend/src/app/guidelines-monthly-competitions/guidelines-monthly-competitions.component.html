GUIDELINES FOR MONTHLY COMPETITIONS




General Guidelines


  1. All songs should be play with Shruthi and Thalam
  2. Stick to the time limit allocated to your age category
  3. Announce only the song name, raga, thala and the composer's name before you start the Performance .
  4. Attire: Traditional Indian outfit (Music: Long skirt for Girls and sari for women & Kurtha or Dhothi for Boys/Men
    Dance: Wear traditional dance dress.)

Audio and Video Guidelines


  1. Please use electronic tanpura/Shruthi box ONLY and put you can use metronome for thalam or anybody can show the thalam.
  2. Microphones are ok to use as long as it is setup properly.
  3. You can use tala app or mridangam for accompaniment
  4. Your audio should be free from all background noise and clear for judges to hear
  5. Please do a test recording before the final version.
  6. No karaoke music allowed for this video recording.
  7. Use a tripod to shoot so there is NO shake in the video.
  8. Video background can be plain/simple and match against the color of your outfit.
  9. Avoid, white and black colors for video quality purposes.
  10. Angle to center of the performer and close enough to see the vocalist's hand thalam
  11. Only LIVE recording is permitted & date of recording should be mentioned.
  12. Video should be in Landscape only and portrait mode will be disqualified.

JUDGMENT


  1. Performance will be judged by senior & leading international music teachers
  2. Once registered, only registration number will be used for judgment
  3. Participation Certificates will be given to all the Participants
  4. Certificates will be issued for each and every category & levels
  5. 1st, 2nd 3rd place Certificates will be issued for international/national/state - for each Category & levels

PRIZES & AWARDS


Winners will receive winning Certificates (Other than the winners, all the participants will receive International Music Competition Participation Certificate.)


  • 1st Prize - Gold Medal & Certificate
  • 2nd Prize - Silver Medal & Certificate
  • 3rd Prize - Bronze Medal & Certificate
  • Consolation - Certificate

Other than winners , all other participants will receive International Music Competition Participation Certificate.
