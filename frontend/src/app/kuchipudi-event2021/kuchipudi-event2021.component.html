INTERNATIONAL AWARD CEREMONY

Renowned violinist Mahamahopadhyaya Padmashree Dr.Annavarapu Ramaswamy On the auspicious occasion of announcing the Padma Shri award to by the government, ICMDA Chennai honored him with the Sangeet Ratna Sagara title. On the same occasion, the founders of the association honored various artists who participated in the Guinness World Record Program. The Pure Gold Medal and Pure Silver Medal was presented as the first and second prize to the winners of the International Music and Dance Competitions organized by the ICMDA and Thyagayya Charitable Trust and Thyagayya TV. Sri Siddhendra Yogi Kalapeetham Principal Dr. Vedanta Ramalingashastri, Radheshyam, Natyacharya Krishnakumar, Dr. Yeleswarapu Srinivasu, Kalaimamani Sri Madhavpeddi Murthy, Founder and General Secretary E.R Janardhan and music and dance teachers are participated. College Principal Vedanta Ramalinga Shastri lauded the initiative taken by the institute in developing the cultural arts. Janardhan said that he is going to held many such great programs in the future. The teachers were presented with the Yasaswi and Sikhamani Awards.
